Our Story

Builtable offers spaces for coworking, private offices, workshops and events in the heart of Mandaluyong City, Philippines.


We want startups to focus on what they do best. We invest on what is necessary by listening to what our customers really need - fast internet (up to 250 Mbps), a conducive environment for work/collaboration, accessible location (5-minutes away from MRT Shaw), flexible open hours and very affordable rates. We want the young makers and artists to thrive; And be in a place where they can freely express their creativity through leading workshops and exhibits. We continue to help businesses from small to big companies (like Vaquform and Microsoft) by providing an events place for their hackathons, townhalls and meetups.

Here in Builtable, our goal is more than just providing space, we empower the small players; We help BUILD the next big names in the Philippines and globally.

5/5, Our Customers love us!
