How to get to Builtable?


MRT: Stop at Shaw Blvd. Station, it's a 5min walk from there!
Shaw Blvd: From Manila, go straight along Shaw Blvd. then take a u-turn below the fly over. From Pasig, take a straight path and go below the fly-over.
EDSA: Northbound, Go straight along EDSA then right to United Street, then left to Sto. Cristo, then another left to Shaw Blvd. For southbound, along EDSA turn right to Shaw Blvd Service Road (Starmall Side)
