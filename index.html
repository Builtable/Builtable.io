ABOUT BUILTABLE

We are a coworking space in Manila for makers and startups; On top of that we provide private rooms, meetings rooms and event spaces.

Built for discovery, passion and innovation. Make it Builtable! The goal of the company is to build a community for innovators and makers. A space to help with their journey of discovery. Enabling innovators by providing them necessary equipment, network and opportunities in making all things Builtable!
